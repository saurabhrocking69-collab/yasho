Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from riders and drivers.

How do I book a ride on Sppero? +

Download the Sppero app, register with your phone number, enter your pickup and drop location, choose your vehicle type, and confirm the booking. A nearby driver is matched instantly and you can track them live.

What payment methods does Sppero accept? +

Sppero accepts cash, UPI/direct payment, online payment via Razorpay, and Sppero Wallet (prepaid balance). You can choose your preferred method before confirming a booking.

How do I join as a Sppero Buddy driver? +

Download the Sppero Buddy app, register with your phone number, and upload your driving licence, vehicle RC, insurance, and a selfie. Our team verifies documents within 24 hours and you can start accepting rides.

When do I get my referral bonus? +

Referral rewards are credited to both you and your friend only after your friend completes their first ride on Sppero. Simply sharing the code or registration is not enough — the first ride must be completed.

How does the driver payout work? +

Earnings are credited to your Sppero Buddy wallet after every completed ride. You can request a payout to your bank account or UPI ID from the app. Sppero processes payouts automatically via Razorpay, typically within a few hours.

Is there a cancellation fee? +

Cancellation is free within 2 minutes of booking or before a driver is assigned. After that, a fee of ₹25–₹50 applies depending on how far the driver has traveled. See our full Cancellation Policy for details.

What is Sppero Wallet cashback? +

When you pay for rides using your Sppero Wallet balance, you earn automatic cashback on every transaction. The cashback is added directly back to your wallet and can be used for future rides.

How do I report a safety issue? +

Open the Sppero app, go to the completed ride, and tap "Report Issue". Select the appropriate category. For urgent safety emergencies, use the SOS button available during active rides. Our safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
