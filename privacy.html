Privacy Policy

How Sppero collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: June 2025  ·  Effective: June 1, 2025

Sppero ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use the Sppero or Sppero Buddy mobile applications and related services ("Platform").

🔒 Your privacy matters to us. We collect only what is necessary to provide our services and never sell your personal data to third parties for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing rides: Matching customers with nearby drivers, real-time tracking, navigation
  • Payments: Processing fares, wallet transactions, driver payouts via Razorpay
  • Safety: Verifying identities, monitoring for suspicious activity, investigating complaints
  • Customer support: Resolving disputes, complaint handling, refund processing
  • Communication: Sending booking confirmations, OTPs, promotional offers (opt-out available)
  • Improvements: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app features and performance
  • Legal compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements under Indian law

3. Location Data

Location data is the most sensitive data we collect. Here is exactly how we use it:

  • Customers: Location is used to find your pickup point, show nearby drivers, and track the ride in progress. Location access ends when the ride is complete.
  • Drivers: Location is used to dispatch ride requests, show customers real-time driver position, and verify ride routes. Location sharing stops when driver goes offline.
  • We do NOT: Share your real-time location with anyone other than the matched driver/customer in an active ride. We do not sell location data to advertisers.
📱 You can control location permissions in your device settings. Disabling location access will prevent core features from working (ride booking, tracking). "While using app" permission is recommended.

4. Sharing Your Information

With Drivers (Customers)

When you book a ride, your first name, pickup location, and drop destination are shared with your matched driver. Your full phone number is masked — drivers see a masked number for in-app calls.

With Customers (Drivers)

When a ride is assigned, your first name, vehicle details, and rating are shared with the customer. Your real phone number is masked for privacy.

With Service Providers

  • Razorpay: Payment processing and driver payouts
  • Firebase (Google): Push notifications for ride updates
  • Google Maps: Navigation and route calculation
  • Cloud hosting: Secure data storage (Railway.app)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority. We will notify you where legally permitted before such disclosure.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in India. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for payment transactions
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (JWT + bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data internally

Despite best efforts, no digital system is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that affects your information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required under applicable law.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a commonly used format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with Sppero's grievance officer or applicable data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sppero.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Tracking

The Sppero mobile app does not use browser cookies. However, our website (sppero.com) may use cookies for analytics and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Our apps use Firebase Analytics (anonymized) to understand usage patterns and improve the app experience.

9. Children's Privacy

Sppero is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@sppero.com.

10. Third-Party Links

The Sppero app may contain links to third-party services (e.g., Google Maps, payment pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notification or SMS. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Sppero after changes constitutes your acceptance.
