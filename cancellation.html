Cancellation Policy

Fair and transparent cancellation rules for riders and drivers on Sppero.

Last Updated: June 2025  ·  Effective Immediately
ℹ️ Quick Summary: Cancel within 2 minutes of booking for free. After that, a small fee applies to respect the driver's time and effort. Hourly bookings have stricter rules once matched.

1. Standard Ride Cancellations (By Customer)

Free Cancellation Window

You may cancel any standard ride booking free of charge within 2 minutes of placing the booking, provided a driver has not yet been assigned or is not yet on the way to your pickup point.

Cancellation Fees

SituationTime WindowFee
Cancelled before driver assignedAny timeFREE
Cancelled within 2 min of booking (driver assigned)0–2 minutesFREE
Cancelled after 2 min (driver on the way)2–5 minutes₹25 fee
Cancelled after driver reaches pickupDriver arrived₹40 fee
No-show (driver waited 5+ minutes)After arrival₹50 fee

Cancellation fees are deducted from your Sppero Wallet or charged to your linked payment method. The driver receives 80% of the cancellation fee as compensation for their time and travel.

2. Hourly Booking Cancellations

Hourly bookings have stricter cancellation terms because drivers plan their entire schedule around these bookings.

SituationFee
Cancelled before any driver is matchedFREE
Cancelled within 5 min of driver matching₹50 fee
Cancelled after driver has reached pickup₹100 fee
Booking cancelled mid-rideFare for hours used
⚠️ For hourly bookings, once the driver has accepted and confirmed the booking, cancellation may not be available through the app. Please contact Sppero Support immediately in such cases.

3. Ride Extensions (Hourly)

During an active hourly booking, customers can request to extend the booking by additional hours. Extension requests must be sent at least 15 minutes before the current booking ends. The driver may accept or decline the extension. If declined, the ride ends as originally scheduled and no fee is charged.

4. Driver Cancellations

Sppero Buddy drivers are expected to complete accepted rides. Excessive cancellations affect their service rating and earnings.

  • Drivers may cancel in genuine emergencies (vehicle breakdown, safety concern, etc.)
  • Cancellation after reaching pickup requires contacting support
  • 3+ driver cancellations in 24 hours: Temporary booking suspension (2 hours)
  • Repeated pattern: Account review and potential deactivation
  • If a driver cancels after reaching pickup, the customer is not charged any fee

5. Ride Not Started — Special Cases

Driver Can't Find Customer

If the driver arrives at the pickup point and cannot locate the customer after waiting 5 minutes and attempting contact via the in-app call, the ride may be cancelled. In this case, a ₹40 no-show fee applies to the customer.

Safety Cancellations

If a customer or driver feels unsafe, they may cancel immediately with no fee from Sppero. Please report safety incidents to Sppero Support immediately for investigation.

6. Refund Policy

Refunds for cancellation fees or disputed charges are processed as follows:

  • Wallet refund: If you paid from Sppero Wallet, the refund is credited back to your wallet within 24 hours.
  • Online payment refund: Refunds to original payment method take 5–7 working days.
  • Disputed charges: Raise a complaint through the app. Sppero Support reviews within 48 hours.
  • Error cancellations: If the app cancelled your ride due to a technical error, you will receive a full refund immediately.

7. Repeat Cancellations by Customers

Frequent cancellations affect the Sppero community and driver livelihoods. The following thresholds apply:

CancellationsPeriodAction
3+ cancellations after driver dispatchPer dayWarning issued
5+ cancellations in a weekPer weekAccount review
Consistent pattern of cancellationsOngoingBooking restrictions

8. How to Cancel a Ride

  • Open the Sppero app and go to the active ride screen
  • Tap the "Cancel Ride" button
  • Select a cancellation reason from the list
  • Confirm cancellation
  • If fee applies, you'll see the amount before confirming

9. Contact & Disputes

If you believe a cancellation fee was charged incorrectly, or your ride was cancelled through no fault of your own, please contact Sppero Support:

  • 📧 Email: support@sppero.com
  • 📱 In-App: Complaints → New Complaint → Payment Issue
  • ⏱️ Response time: Within 24–48 hours
📋 Sppero reserves the right to update this cancellation policy. You will be notified of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Sppero after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
